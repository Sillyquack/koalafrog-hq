## Scientific and evidence boundaries

Benchmark observations are not targets. Targets are not Formula Lines. Functional requirements are not ingredients. Reference candidates are not Workspace Ingredients, Supplier Products, or stock. Unknown remains Unknown.

Research does not generate formula percentages, phases, process instructions, procurement, supplier assumptions, compatibility, efficacy, safety, or regulatory conclusions. Draft Formula creation remains blank.

Beard Studio grooming observations, measurements, tool capabilities, procedures, and Product usage snapshots remain separate from Formula facts. They do not create Ingredients, Formula Lines, percentages, phases, supplier records, inventory assumptions, efficacy claims, safety conclusions, or regulatory conclusions.

## Acceptance testing

The following workflow was manually verified:

Product Studio → Design Natural Deodorant → Benchmark Lab → save research → Continue research → Functional Requirement Importance → Review status → Formula Readiness → blank Draft Formula → Continue to formulation → Natural Deodorant Studio.

- Importance alone does not complete readiness.
- Reviewed completes the Functional Requirement readiness check.
- Research needed does not complete it.
- Explicit Not applicable counts as reviewed.
- Save and refresh preserve both values.
- Research Progress updates correctly.

## Known limitations

- Workspace test records are not automatically deleted and remain available for deliberate cleanup through the UI.
- The former `koalafrog-hq:beard-studio:v1` browser-storage key is not imported automatically. It is left untouched to avoid guessing whether it contains authoritative user data; v0.13.0 does not read or write it.
- Functional Requirement cards may later benefit from a compact or collapsible presentation.
- Recorded open questions and unresolved requirements are separate concepts.

## Persistence and schema

- Workspace schema advances from v9 to `koalafrog-hq:workspace:v10`; legacy workspaces hydrate a deliberate empty Beard Studio aggregate without mutating their source.
- Backup format remains `koalafrog-backup-v1` and now includes the typed Beard Studio aggregate.
- Hosted Beard Studio uses the startup-selected Supabase workspace repository and the shared workspace action executor; it never falls back to browser storage.
- Follow-up migrations enforce workspace-scoped attachment, session, log, and Product relationships and add an aggregate revision guard for stale writes.
- No remote database migrations were run during release preparation.
