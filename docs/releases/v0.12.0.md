## Evidence and claims boundary

Natural Deodorant records development intent and physical observations. It does not claim antiperspirant performance, prevention of sweating, antibacterial action, timed protection, safety, stability, compliance, or launch readiness. Odour-control observations do not establish efficacy. Bicarbonate-free intent does not imply hypoallergenic or irritation-free.

Unknown melting, addition, filling, usage, physical-form, and packaging limits remain unknown until supported by supplier information or controlled evidence. Packaging intent does not establish stock, and no planning or Formula handoff creates Inventory records or movements.
