# Procurement and equipment readiness

Before approving a Purchase Plan, confirm the exact Supplier Products, quantities, MOQ/order multiples, quote validity, currency, known landed-cost components, and lead times. Unknown shipping, duties, tax, fees, or exchange rates must remain visibly unknown.

Before Production, confirm the exact Approved Formula Version, usable raw-material and packaging stock, required equipment type and capability, capacity and precision, item status, and current service evidence. A warning is a prompt for human review; it is never cleared by invented data.

The deterministic Procurement Copilot surfaces expiring quotes, unreviewed candidates, open external-order receipt work, and equipment evidence gaps. It performs no model call and writes nothing.

Backup exports include Phase 10A relational records. Private document binaries remain outside JSON and require the separate authenticated Storage export described in `BACKUP_AND_RESTORE.md`.
