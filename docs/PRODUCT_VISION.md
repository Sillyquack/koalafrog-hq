# Product vision

Koalafrog HQ is the private operating system for taking Koalafrog products from a half-formed idea to a repeatable, documented, launch-ready object. It should preserve both the hard evidence of development and the softer context behind decisions: what was tried, what changed, what was noticed, and why a direction was chosen.

The system is designed for one owner. Depth, continuity, and workshop fluency matter more than collaboration theatre or public-facing presentation.

## Long-term workspace

- **Product development:** product briefs, stages, decisions, milestones, and portfolio context.
- **Formulas:** controlled versions, percentages, scaling, comparisons, and promotion history.
- **Ingredients:** material identity, working observations, suppliers, lots, costs, and documentation.
- **Scent development:** scent profiles, individual materials, accords, experiments, and the evolving signature DNA.
- **Lab batches:** exact weigh-ins, process steps, yield, deviations, photos, and timed observations.
- **Inventory:** lots, locations, stock movements, reorder awareness, expiry, and finished goods.
- **Production:** validated run sheets, production orders, batch genealogy, and release records.
- **Testing:** protocols, stability schedules, wear trials, packaging compatibility, and comparable results.
- **Suppliers:** contacts, quotes, lead times, samples, minimums, and sourcing history.
- **Equipment:** a workshop register, calibration, maintenance, and operating methods.
- **Costing:** formula, packaging, labour, overhead, scenario, and margin models.
- **Compliance:** structured documentation, review status, market requirements, and product files.
- **Packaging:** components, specifications, artwork, compatibility trials, and vendors.
- **Launch planning:** readiness gates, dependencies, dates, assets, and launch decisions.
- **Knowledge management:** linked research, procedures, decisions, references, and lessons learned.

Over time, Koalafrog HQ should become institutional memory for a company that is still small enough to care about every gram and every decision.
